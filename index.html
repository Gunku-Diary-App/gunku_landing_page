Features

The Gunku app helps you write great diaries. With the Gunku application, you can write the diary of the day we are in. This part is important because the Gunku app doesn't want you to write diary to yesterday or tomorrow. You can only write today's diary today. But you can read the diary of the day you want on the days you want.


You can easily protect your diary with a password. The Gunku app is the easiest and most simple diary app to use. In no way does it share your data with others. It saves your data encrypted in your device. You can permanently delete this data at any time.
